IEEE invites professionals, researchers, industry leaders, and students to join for an engaging Information and Networking Event:

The Future of Engineering in the Age of Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence is transforming every facet of modern life. Engineering stands at the forefront of this revolution. This event will explore how AI is reshaping engineering disciplines — from design and manufacturing to communications, energy, health, and beyond. Attendees will gain insights into emerging trends, innovative applications, and the ethical considerations that will define the coming era of technological advancement.

a dynamic program of a keynote talk, an interactive panel discussion, and networking opportunities, participants will:
  • Hear from experts on the impact of AI across diverse professional fields
  • Explore how engineers can leverage AI to solve global challenges
  • Engage in conversations about AI-driven engineering
  • Connect with peers, mentors, and professionals to build valuable networks

This event offers a unique forum to learn, share, and imagine the future of engineering in an age where human ingenuity and artificial intelligence intersect. Whether you are an industry professional seeking to adapt to rapid change, a researcher pushing the boundaries of discovery, or a student eager to explore career pathways, this gathering will provide inspiration, knowledge, and community.

  • Date: October 30, 2025
  • Time: 5:00 to 8:30 p.m.
  • Location: Forge Event Center Former Loveland HP 815 14th St SW Bldg B120, Loveland, CO 80537
  • Food and Beverage Service Available at Grimm Brothers Brewhouse
